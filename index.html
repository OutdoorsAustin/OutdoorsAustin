Zilker Botanical Garden

The Zilker Botanical Garden is a botanical garden of varied topography located on the south bank of the Colorado River at 2220 Barton Springs Road, near downtown Austin, Texas, USA
Zilker Garden Website

Tips:

  • Wear appropiate hiking shoes
  • Bring water
  • If you're bringing your dog, try to bring them early in the morning or on an a evening. It can get pretty hot for their paws

Mount Bonnel

Mount Bonnell, also known as Covert Park, is a prominent point alongside the Lake Austin portion of the Colorado River in Austin, Texas. It has been a popular tourist destination since the 1850s.
Mount Bonnell Website

Tips:

  • Must wear tennis shoes
  • Bring water to stay hydrated

Kayaking at Lady bird Lake

Lady Bird Lake is a reservoir on the Colorado River in Downtown Austin, Texas, in the United States.
Lady Bird Lake Website

Tips:

  • Most kayaking services will ask for a valid Driver's License so make sure you bring that with you.
  • Bring Sunscreen!
