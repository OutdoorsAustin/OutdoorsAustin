Barton Creek Greenbelt Trail

Barton Creek Greenbelt is so beloved that locals commonly refer to it as “the Greenbelt,” as if it were the only one. Located in south-central Austin, the Barton Creek Greenbelt contains 12.68 miles of gorgeous trails, with the main trail spanning about 7 miles. This particular greenbelt is considered one of the top hiking trails in Texas.
Barton Creek Greenbelt Trail Website

Wild Basin Wilderness Preserve

Wild Basin Wilderness Preserve consists of 227 acres of wood west of Austin, Texas. The preserve was founded in 1974. The preserve maintains 2.5 miles of hiking trails
